Synopsis

A thrilling mystery set in the mountains of Switzerland, where secrets echo through generations. When journalist Emma Reeves returns to her ancestral village to investigate a series of strange disappearances, she uncovers a web of lies that has silenced the town for decades. As the truth emerges, so does a deadly threat determined to keep the past buried.
